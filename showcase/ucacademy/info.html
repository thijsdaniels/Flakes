    • What is a day at UC Academy like?
      Every day consists of four time-slots, each two hours long, with breaks in between. At the start of every time-slot the teacher lectures on one topic, focusing on the following:
      • Giving a concise and structured overview of the material, distinguishing what is important and what is less important.
      • Which questions are often asked on the exams and how to recognize them.
      • What mistakes are often made and how to prevent making them.
      The time that is left over will be spent making exercises based on previous IB exams. Whenever you get stuck on a question there will always be a teacher available there to help you. After all, there are only five students to one teacher.

      Every course has a slightly different schedule, please go to subjects to get more information.
    • Does UC Academy offer any housing for the duration of the course period?
      Unfortunately, we are currently unable to offer housing to students taking a program with UC Academy. We advise to make use of friends, colleagues and relatives in the area for a place to stay.

      We can point you to a few hostels and hotels that are quite close, but we cannot guarantee that they will have rooms available during the course period (prices are indicative only):
      • Stone Hotel & Hostel
        Private: 55 euros, dorms: 21 euros.
      • Hotel Oorsprongpark
        Private: 75 euros, double: 45 euros.
      • Sandton Malie Hotel
        Private: 110 euros.
      Some of our students commute between home and Utrecht as public transport to University College is excellent.
    • Who are the teachers?
      All our teachers are elite IB alumni that graduated top of their class. They are studying at University College Utrecht, Netherlands' highest ranking bachelor program, where they deepen and broaden their knowledge on their subjects. Our teachers are young and smart, they have been where you are now and have everything to help you achieve the level you wish for.
    • How do you teach the entire syllabus in such a short time?
      We spent our time analyzing what students struggle with most so that we can teach efficiently. We have prepared summary theory lectures where we address pitfalls and supply strategies to answer IB exam questions. Immediately after our students apply their newly learnt knowledge to past IB exam questions.
    • After following a crash course, will I be fully prepared for the final exam?
      After 4 days of intensive revision you will be much better prepared than you ever were before. During the course it will become apparent what topics you master and where you can improve. Our teachers can help you set up an individual learning strategy at the end of the course so you can spend your time most efficienty in order to get the maximum improvement of your grade.
    • What is the size of the classes?
      We strive to maintain a maximum of five students per teacher in order to guarantee ample attention to every student's individual learning needs. Naturally, classes may be larger than 5 students, but then there will consequently be more teachers.
    • When do courses take place?
      Each course is taught over two of the four periods, generally once as higher level and once as lower level. The dates of the course-periods are as follows:
      • Period 1: March 22-25
      • Period 2: April 6-9
      • Period 3: April 12-16 *
      • Period 4: April 27-30
      * Higher level Economics will start on the 12th of April, while higher level physics will start on the 13th.

      You can subscribe to one course in each period. If the courses you wish to follow are taught in the same period, please contact us and we will try to find a solution. For further information, refer to the schedule on the dates page.
    • Where do courses take place?
      You will be studying at the beautiful and historic campus of University College Utrecht. UCU is the honors college of Utrecht University. The residential campus houses around 700 students from all over the world. Besides providing an inspiring setting, the campus is easily accessible by public transport. The campus address is Campusplein 1, 3584ED in Utrecht.

    • What should I bring?
      For the duration of the course you will need everything you regularly need for a class. Pens, pencils, a notebook, a calculator, et cetera. For a detailed list, visit the subjects page. Most importantly, we recommend you show up well rested. Covering all the essential information in 4 days requires 100% of your energy and mental capacity. Lunch, snacks and refreshements will be provided throughout the day. If you are planning on staying in the city of Utrecht please remember to bring some extra money for breakfast.
    • How much does a four day course cost?
      A four day revision course costs 480 euros. This includes up to 36 hours of guidance and teaching, course material, lunch, dinner and snacks. We are offering a 20% early-bird discount until the 5th of March, that means you pay only 384 euros!
    • How do I register for a course?
      Simply go to the sign up page and fill in the online application form. Your registration is final only after payment is received. Payment can be made by bank transfer and needs to be done in advance.
    • What are my payment options?
      You must pay by bank transfer before the revision course starts. You will receive an email with payment instructions when you sign up.
    • When can I register for a course?
      Registration takes place all year round, there is no deadline. However, it is smart to register early because we have limited capacity. There is no deadline for registration, you can see which courses still have openings on the dates page.
    • Can I sign up with a friend?
      Certainly. You can request things like these by leaving a comment when signing up, or, if you're already registered, you can contact us to inform us of your wishes.
    • I can't find the subject I'd like to follow!
      Unfortunately we have a limited selection of courses available. The courses we offer depend on supply and demand, but also on how effective we believe a four day revision course would be.
    • I would like to follow two courses, but they are offered at the same time.
      Please contact us stating which courses you would like to follow. We will try to find a solution depending on the situation.
    • Can I follow multiple courses during one period?
      No, due to the intensity of the courses you can only attend a single course each period. After following a course you are almost fully prepared for your final exam.
    • I have a different question!
      Please contact us with any questions you might have.
