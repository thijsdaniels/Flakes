      1. attending all classes during the Course for which they have enrolled;
      2. not possessing or consuming alcohol or any illegal substance for the duration of the Course, on or off the Course location;
      3. treating others, including Our staff, Our teachers and other students, with respect, courtesy and civility; and
      4. complying with day-to-day rules and instructions of our staff.
    4. If a Student fails to comply with clause 2 b-c above, we reserve the right to suspend Students from teaching at our exclusive discretion.
    5. We are not responsible for insuring the Student. The Guardian is advised to ensure that Students are adequately insured.
    6. The Guardian consents to:
      1. the Student leaving the Course Location alone or with other Students outside of teaching time and that they do so entirely at their own risk and responsibility; and

  4. The Right to Cancel
    1. The Student can, within 14 days from the days from the day of agreement of these terms and conditions, cancel the agreement. The Student must in that case leave or send a message about this to Us. Right to cancel is not applicable 7 days before the course will start.
    2. If the Student chooses to activate his or her right to cancel, We shall, within 30 days, repay any fee paid by the Student to Us.
  5. Payment, Cancellation, Refunds and Termination
    1. The fee will be specified in an email sent to the email address specified by the Student or Guardian in the registration process. The Student or Guardian is responsible for providing a correct and functioning email address, and for checking this continuously up to the start of the course.
    2. The Course fees must be paid within 14 days after registration or before the course starts. All payments must be made in time.
    3. For any late payments, we will charge interest at the rate of LIBOR + 5%, accruing on daily basis from the last day of payment as specified above until the fee is paid.
    4. If the Student cancels the registration more than 14 days after his or her registration, we will charge an administrative fee of 25 % of the course fee. If the Student cancels the registration after the 28th of February 2012, the Student is, regardless of cancellation, eligible for payment of the full course fee and no repayment of fees will be enacted, if the cancellation is not made within 14 days of registration.
    5. If the student should become ill before the start of the courses, they reserve the right to cancel the agreement. An administrative fee of 25% will be charged of the course fee. To use the right of this clause the following condition must be met:
      1. A statement from a doctor must be emailed to Us;
      2. The doctor must also fill in and email a form provided by Us.
      We shall then within 30 days repay 75% of the course fee to the Student.
    6. Regardless of what is stated elsewhere in these terms & conditions, we reserve the right to cancel a course in the unusual case of having 3 or less registered participants. In the case of a cancellation, we will notify the student no later than 21 days prior to the courses.
    7. If we receive neither a payment nor a cancellation, we will charge the full Course fee and interest as in clause 5c above.
    8. If the Student voluntarily withdraws from the Course, no part of the fees will be refunded, alternatively, if the Course fee has not yet been paid, the full course fee and interest as in clause 5c above will be charged.
    9. If the student's visa application is rejected and the student is prevented from entering the country, it is not grounds for refund of tuition costs.
    10. If a Student breaches these Terms & Conditions or otherwise engages in any activity or behaviour that in our judgement undermines, or is likely to undermine, the safe and effective running of the Course or that of other courses simultaneously held by Us, we will at our exclusive discretion ask the Student to withdraw from the Course. In that event:
      1. the Guardian will be immediately informed;
      2. the Guardian must arrange and pay for the Student to return home; and
      3. no refunds of any part of the fee will be given, alternatively, if the Course fee has not yet been paid, the full course fee and interest according to clause 5c above will be charged.
